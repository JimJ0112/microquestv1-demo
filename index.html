About Us

microQuest is a system that is capable to provide a list of jobs and services. There are two different kinds of user accounts: the responder, who can work on requests, and the requestor, who can make requests and request assistance. Home services is a job that can be done using physical self, such as doing laundry, washing plates, cleaning the house, and sometimes gardening. Computer-related tasks that require knowledge of computers, can be done at home, data entry, encoding, and installing software. In PasaBuy, any goods you want to be delivered to your house.
